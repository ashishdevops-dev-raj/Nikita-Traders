Company Profile
Nikita Traders

Nikita Traders is a trusted sole proprietorship established in 2009 under the name Nikita Travel. Initially focused on transport services, the firm expanded operations and, after GST amendments, was rebranded as Nikita Traders as a diversified multi-sector service provider.

We started with cab and bus rental and evolved into integrated transport and supply solutions. Today, we serve businesses at a Pan India level across Telecom, FMCG, Healthcare, and Corporate sectors with reliable delivery and long-term service partnerships.
